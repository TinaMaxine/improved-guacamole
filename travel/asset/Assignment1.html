Italy – A Slice Of Medieval Life

During this time, I was thrilled to find near-empty beaches. The Alba White Truffle Festival and the popular EuroChocolate from Perugia attract a large majority of foodies from all over the world. My first foray into Italy was Lugano. Though a very fancy part of the country, what impressed me most was the food :) Due to time constraints, I could not venture much into the country until last year, when I planned a weekend getaway to Naples. Man, was I hooked! Home to the world’s best coffee, pizza, pasta and cheese, all you need to pack up on your trip to Napoli is an empty and stretchy belly. When you are travelling, make sure to pack a lot of woolens and bring your near and dear ones to this beautiful destination.
